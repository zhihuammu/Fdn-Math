1. Challenging Exercise 1#

Exercise 1.1

Expand and simplify the following expressions.

  1. \((a+b)(a-b)\)

  2. \((a+b)^2\)

  3. \((a-b)^2\)

  4. \((a+b)(a^2-ab+b^2)\)

  5. \((a-b)(a^2+ab+b^2)\)

  6. \((a+b+c)^2\)

  7. \((a+b+c)(a^2+b^2+c^2-ab-bc-ca)\)

  8. \((a+b)(m+n)\)

Exercise 1.2

Suppose \(a\), \(b\) and \(c\) are three sides of a triangle \(\triangle{ABC}\), and they satisfy the equation

\[a^2+b^2+c^2=ab+bc+ca\]

What type of the triangle \(\triangle{ABC}\) is?

  1. Right-angle triangle

  2. Isosceles triangle

  3. Equilateral triangle

Exercise 1.3

Factorise the following expressions.

  1. \(2ax-10ay+5by-bx\)

  2. \(a^2-ab+ac-bc\)

  3. \(xy-x-y+1\)

  4. \(x^2-y^2+ax+ay\)

  5. \(a^2-2ab+b^2-c^2\)

  6. \(x^2-x-9y^2-3y\)

  7. \(x^2-y^2-z^2-2yz\)

  8. \(x^3+x^2y-xy^2-y^3\)

  9. \(ax^2-bx^2+bx-ax+a-b\)

  10. \(x^2+6xy+9y^2-16a^2+8a-1\)

  11. \(a^2-6ab+12b+9b^2-4a\)

  12. \(a^4-2a^3+a^2-9\)

  13. \(4a^2x-4a^2y-b^2x+b^2y\)

  14. \(x^2-2xy-xz+yz+y^2\)

  15. \(a^2-2a+b^2-2b+2ab+1\)

  16. \((a+c)(a-c)+b(b-2a)\)

Exercise 1.4

Factorise the following expressions.

  1. \(3x^2-11x+10\)

    Solution

    \(3x^2-11x+10=(3x-5)(x-2)\)

  2. \(15x^2+7xy-4y^2\)

    Solution

    \(15x^2+7xy-4y^2=(3x-y)(5x+4y)\)

  3. \(a^2x^2-6ax+8\)

    Solution

    \(a^2x^2-6ax+8=(ax-2)(ax-4)\)

  4. \(m^2-4mn+4n^2-3m+6n+2\)

    Solution
    \[\begin{align*} ~& m^2-4mn+4n^2-3m+6n+2\\ = &(m-2n)^2-3(m-2n)+2\\ = &(m-2n-1)(m-2n-2) \end{align*}\]

  5. \(4x^2-4xy-6x+3y+y^2-10\)

    Solution
    \[\begin{align*} ~&4x^2-4xy-6x+3y+y^2-10 \\ = &(2x-y)^2-3(2x-y)-10 \\ = &(2x-y-5)(2x-y+2) \end{align*}\]

  6. \(12(x+y)^2+11(x^2-y^2)+2(x-y)^2\)

    Solution
    \[\begin{align*} ~& 12(x+y)^2+11(x^2-y^2)+2(x-y)^2 \\ = &\left[3(x+y)+2(x-y)\right] \left[4(x+y)+(x-y)\right] \\ = &(5x+y)(5x+3y) \end{align*}\]

  7. \(abcx^2+(a^2b^2+c^2)x+abc\)

    Solution

    \(abcx^2+(a^2b^2+c^2)x+abc=(abx+c)(cx+ab)\)

  8. \(2005x^2-(2005^2-1)x-2005\)

    Solution

    \(2005x^2-(2005^2-1)x-2005=(2005x+1)(x-2005)\)

  9. \((x+1)(x+2)(x+3)(x+6)+x^2\)

    Solution
    \[\begin{align*} ~&(x+1)(x+2)(x+3)(x+6)+x^2 \\ = &(x^2+7x+6)(x^2+5x+6)+x^2 \\ = &(x^2+5x+6)^2+2x(x^2+5x+6)+x^2 \\ = &(x^2+5x+6+x)^2\\ = &(x^2+6x+6)^2 \end{align*}\]

  10. \((x^2+xy+y^2)^2-4xy(x^2+y^2)\)

    Solution
    \[\begin{align*} ~&(x^2+xy+y^2)^2-4xy(x^2+y^2)\\ = &[(x^2+y^2)+xy]^2-4xy(x^2+y^2) \\ = &(x^2+y^2)^2+2xy(x^2+y^2)+(xy)^2-4xy(x^2+y^2)\\ = &(x^2+y^2)-2xy(x^2+y^2)+(xy)^2\\ = &(x^2+y^2-xy)^2 \end{align*}\]

  11. \((x^2+3x+2)(4x^2+8x+3)-90\)

    Solution
    \[\begin{align*} ~&(x^2+3x+2)(4x^2+8x+3)-90 \\ = &(x+1)(x+2)(2x+3)(2x+1)-90 \\ = &(2x^2+5x+3)(2x^2+5x+2)-90 \\ = &(2x^2+5x+2)^2 + (2x^2+5x+2) -90 \\ = &(2x^2+5x+2+10)(2x^2+5x+2-9) \\ = &(2x^2+5x+12)(2x^2+5x-7)\\ = &(2x^2+5x+12)(2x+7)(x-1) \end{align*}\]

  12. \((a^2+1)^2+(a^2+5)^2-4(a^2+3)^2\)

    Solution
    \[\begin{align*} ~& (a^2+1)^2+(a^2+5)^2-4(a^2+3)^2 \\ = &(a^2+1)^2+[(a^2+5)+2(a^2+3)][(a^2+5)-2(a^2+3)] \\ = &(a^2+1)^2+(3a^2+11)(-a^2-1) \\ = &(a^2+1)(a^2+1-3a^2-11) \\ = &(a^2+1)(-2a^2-10) \\ = &-2(a^2+1)(a^2+5) \end{align*}\]

Exercise 1.5 (Factorise the following expressions.)

  1. \(x^3-9x+8\)

    Solution
    \[\begin{align*} ~& x^3-9x+8\\ = &x^3-1-9x+9 \\ = &(x^3-1)-9(x-1)\\ = &(x-1)(x^2+x+1)-9(x-1)\\ = &(x-1)(x^2+x-8) \end{align*}\]

  2. \((x+1)^4+(x^2-1)^2+(x-1)^4\)

    Solution
    \[\begin{align*} ~& (x+1)^4 + (x^2-1)^2 + (x-1)^4 \\ = &(x+1)^4+\left[(x+1)(x-1)\right]^2+(x-1)^4 \\ = &(x+1)^4+2\left[(x+1)(x-1)\right]^2+(x-1)^4-\left[(x+1)(x-1)\right]^2 \\ = &\left[(x+1)^2+(x-1)^2\right]^2-\left[(x+1)(x-1)\right]^2 \\ = &(2x^2+2+x^2-1)(2x^2+2-x^2+1) \\ = &(3x^2+1)(x^2+3) \end{align*}\]

  3. \(x^3+4x^2-7x+2\)

    Solution
    \[\begin{align*} ~& x^3+4x^2-7x+2 \\ = &(x^3-1)+(4x^2-7x+3)\\ = &(x-1)(x^2+x+1)+(4x-3)(x-1) \\ = & (x-1)(x^2+x+1+4x-3)\\ = & (x-1)(x^2+5x-2) \end{align*}\]

  4. \(a^4+b^4+c^4-2a^2b^2-2a^2c^2-2b^2c^2\)

    Solution
    \[\begin{align*} ~& a^4+b^4+c^4-2a^2b^2-2a^2c^2-2b^2c^2\\ = &(a^2-b^2-c^2)^2-4b^2c^2 \\ = &(a^2-b^2-c^2+2bc)(a^2-b^2-c^2-2bc) \\ = &[a^2-(b-c)^2][a^2-(b+c)^2] \\ = &(a+b-c)(a-b+c)(a+b+c)(a-b-c) \end{align*}\]

  5. \(x^4-7x^2+1\)

    Solution
    \[\begin{align*} ~& x^4-7x^2+1 = &x^4+2x^2+1-9x^2\\ = &(x^2+1)^2-(3x)^2 \\ = &(x^2+1-3x)(x^2+1+3x) \\ = &(x^2+3x+1)(x^2-3x+1) \end{align*}\]

1.1. Solutions to Challenging Exercise 1#

Solution to Exercise 1.1

  1. \((a+b)(a-b)=a^2-b^2\)

  2. \((a+b)^2=a^2+2ab+b^2\)

  3. \((a-b)^2=a^2-2ab+b^2\)

  4. \((a+b)(a^2-ab+b^2)=a^3+b^3\)

  5. \((a-b)(a^2+ab+b^2)=a^3-b^3\)

  6. \[\begin{split} \begin{align*} ~&(a+b+c)^2\\ =&a^2+b^2+c^2+2ab+2bc+2ac \end{align*} \end{split}\]
  7. \[\begin{align*} ~&(a+b+c)(a^2+b^2+c^2-ab-bc-ca) \\ =&a^3+b^3+c^3-3abc \end{align*}\]

  8. \((a+b)(m+n)=am+an+bm+bn\)

Solution to Exercise 1.2

\[\begin{split} \begin{aligned} ~ & \quad a^2+b^2+c^2=ab+bc+ca \\ \Rightarrow & \quad 2a^2+2b^2+2c^2=2ab+2bc+2ca \\ \Rightarrow & \quad a^2+b^2-2ab + a^2+c^2-2ca + b^2+c^2-2bc= 0 \\ \Rightarrow & \quad (a-b)^2+(a-c)^2+(b-c)^2= 0 \\ \Rightarrow & \quad a=b=c \end{aligned} \end{split}\]

\(\therefore \triangle{ABC}\) is an equilateral triangle.

Solution to Exercise 1.3

  1. \[\begin{split} \begin{align*} ~&2ax-10ay+5by-bx \\ =&2a(x-5y)-b(x-5y)\\ =&(2a-b)(x-5y) \end{align*} \end{split}\]
  2. \[\begin{split} \begin{align*} ~&a^2-ab+ac-bc\\ =&a(a-b)+c(a-b) \\ =&(a+c)(a-b) \end{align*} \end{split}\]
  3. \[\begin{split} \begin{align*} ~ & xy-x-y+1 \\ = & x(y-1)-(y-1)\\ = &(x-1)(y-1) \end{align*} \end{split}\]
  4. \[\begin{split} \begin{align*} ~ & x^2-y^2+ax+ay \\ = &(x-y)(x+y)+a(x+y) \\ = &(x+y)(x-y+a) \end{align*} \end{split}\]
  5. \[\begin{split} \begin{align*} ~ & a^2-2ab+b^2-c^2 \\ = &(a-b)^2-c^2\\ = &(a-b+c)(a-b-c) \end{align*} \end{split}\]
  6. \[\begin{split} \begin{align*} ~& x^2-x-9y^2-3y\\ =&(x^2-9y^2)-(x+3y)\\ =&(x-3y)(x+3y)-(x+3y)\\ =&(x-3y-1)(x+3y) \end{align*} \end{split}\]
  7. \[\begin{split} \begin{align*} ~& x^2-y^2-z^2-2yz\\ = &x^2-(y^2+2yz+z^2)\\ = &x^2-(y+z)^2\\ = &(x+y+z)(x-y-z) \end{align*} \end{split}\]
  8. \[\begin{split} \begin{align*} ~& x^3+x^2y-xy^2-y^3\\ = &x^2(x+y)-y^2(x+y)\\ = &(x^2-y^2)(x+y)\\ = &(x-y)(x+y)^2 \end{align*} \end{split}\]
  9. \[\begin{split} \begin{align*} ~ & ax^2-bx^2+bx-ax+a-b \\ = &x^2(a-b)-x(a-b)+(a-b)\\ = &(a-b)(x^2-x+1) \end{align*} \end{split}\]
  10. \[\begin{split} \begin{align*} ~ & x^2+6xy+9y^2-16a^2+8a-1 \\ = &(x+3y)^2-(4a-1)^2\\ = &(x+3y+4a-1)(x+3y-4a+1) \end{align*} \end{split}\]
  11. \[\begin{split} \begin{align*} ~& a^2-6ab+12b+9b^2-4a\\ = &(a^2-6ab+9b^2)-4(a-3b)\\ = &(a-3b)^2-4(a-3b)\\ = &(a-3b)(a-3b-4) \end{align*} \end{split}\]
  12. \[\begin{split} \begin{align*} ~& a^4-2a^3+a^2-9\\ = &(a^2-a)^2-9\\ = &(a^2-a+3)(a^2-a-3) \end{align*} \end{split}\]
  13. \[\begin{split} \begin{align*} ~& 4a^2x-4a^2y-b^2x+b^2y \\ = &4a^2(x-y)-b^2(x-y)\\ = &(x-y)(2a+b)(2a-b) \end{align*} \end{split}\]
  14. \[\begin{split} \begin{align*} ~& x^2-2xy-xz+yz+y^2 \\ = &(x-y)^2-z(x-y) \\ = &(x-y)(x-y-z) \end{align*} \end{split}\]
  15. \[\begin{split} \begin{align*} ~& a^2-2a+b^2-2b+2ab+1 \\ = &a^2+2ab+b^2-2a-2b+1\\ = &(a+b)^2-2(a+b)+1 \\ = &(a+b-1)^2 \end{align*} \end{split}\]
  16. \[\begin{split} \begin{align*} ~ & (a+c)(a-c)+b(b-2a)\\ = &a^2-c^2+b^2-2ab\\ = &(a^2-2ab+b^2)-c^2\\ = &(a-b+c)(a-b-c) \end{align*} \end{split}\]
